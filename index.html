Welcome to Platteville Music Boosters

About Us

At Platteville Music Boosters, we're all about coming together as a community to support music education. We're here to help our local students explore their musical talents and reach their full potential through shared efforts and support. We believe in exposing students to a variety of musical experiences, from performances and workshops to collaborations with visiting professionals, to enrich their learning and inspire a lifelong love of music.

What We Do

Your donation directly supports music in Platteville. Because of your generosity, the Platteville Music Boosters can continue funding for:

  • Scholarships for college, music camps, honors ensembles
  • Travel assistance for performing groups
  • Support for visiting professional performances
  • Assisting with Solo and Ensemble accompanists
  • Financial support for special requests from the music staff
  • Attract and retain top talent music educators
  • Much, much more
