About Us

At Platteville Music Boosters, we're all about coming together as a community to support music education. We're here to help our local students explore their musical talents and reach their full potential.

What We Do

Your donation directly supports music in Platteville. Because of your generosity, the Platteville Music Boosters can continue funding for:

  • Scholarships for college, music camps, honors ensembles
  • Travel assistance for performing groups
  • Support for visiting professional performances
  • Assisting with Solo and Ensemble accompanists
  • Financial support for special requests from the music staff
  • Attract and retain top talent music educators
  • Much, much more
